About

What is OWASP?

The Open Worldwide Application Security Project (OWASP) is a nonprofit foundation well-known for being a long-living group of security professionals and enthusiasts working together to improve applications' security.

  • Open-source software projects
  • Local chapters (hundreds worldwide)
  • Educational and training conferences, including Regional AppSec Days

About AppSec Days Portugal

AppSec Days Portugal 2026 brings together Portugal's AppSec community to share cutting-edge ideas and innovation. We're creating a top-tier, forward-thinking meetup for leaders, architects, developers and other AppSec minds.
